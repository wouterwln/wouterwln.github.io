## International Workshop on Active Inference 2024: Best Talk Award

This year, I had the privilege of attending the *International Workshop on Active Inference (IWAI) 2024*, held at the historic Corpus Christi College in Oxford. It was an incredible opportunity to immerse myself in the global Active Inference community, connecting with researchers and practitioners from around the world.

At the conference, I presented my [paper](https://wouterwln.github.io/publication/iwai-rxenvironments/), which introduces *RxEnvironments.jl* as a package to create digital twin environments for Active Inference research. The reception to my work was incredibly positive, and I was honored to receive the Best Talk Award, an exciting recognition that reinforces the importance and impact of this research.

Overall, the experience was both inspiring and motivating. I left with new insights and inspiration.
